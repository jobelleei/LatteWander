Café Background

Also Coffee in Bacolod is a cozy and minimalist café offering a distinct coffee experience for locals and visitors alike. Known for its warm atmosphere, the café is a haven for coffee lovers seeking quality brews and a relaxed environment. It features a menu of thoughtfully crafted coffee blends and beverages, often paired with light snacks or pastries to enhance the experience. The interior design focuses on simplicity and comfort, making it an inviting space to unwind or catch up with friends. As part of Bacolod's vibrant coffee scene, Also Coffee stands out as a go-to spot for both casual coffee drinkers and enthusiasts​.
