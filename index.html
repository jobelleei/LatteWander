About Us

Latte Wander was founded by two persons who enjoy drinking coffee and decided to hunt for cafes throughout Bacolod City in order to introduce local coffee shops and highlight great places with beautiful ambiance. The hunt for cafe also helps local businesses by advertising their top sellers and addresses. Photos taken from the coffee shops are also released for media purposes, allowing visitors to view the places and spots of the coffee shops. Join us and start Cafe Hunting!
