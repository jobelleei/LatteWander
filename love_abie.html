LOVE, ABIE

Love, Abie Cafe, 13th Lacson St, Bacolod, 6100 Negros Occidental
@loveabieph
Café Background

Love, Abie Café in Bacolod is a charming, Barbie-inspired café that brings a playful and vibrant atmosphere to the city. With its pink-themed decor and whimsical touches, the café offers a unique setting for those seeking both delicious coffee and a fun, Instagram-worthy experience. The café serves freshly brewed coffee made from the finest beans, alongside a selection of mouthwatering pastries and treats, all crafted to perfection. Its cozy and inviting ambiance makes it a popular spot for friends, families, and anyone looking to add a little magic to their day. Love, Abie blends style and flavor, offering a truly memorable experience with every cup and bite.
