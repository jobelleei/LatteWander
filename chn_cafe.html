@chncafe
Café Background

CHN Café, located at the corner of 23rd and Lacson Streets in Brgy 5, Bacolod City, offers a cozy and welcoming space for coffee enthusiasts and casual diners. Known for its freshly crafted coffee and delightful pastries, the café creates a charming atmosphere ideal for relaxing or catching up with friends. Operating from 9 AM to 5 PM daily, CHN Café emphasizes quality and comfort, making it a popular spot for locals and visitors alike. Its convenient location within the city ensures easy access, while the warm service adds to the memorable experience. Whether you're seeking a quiet corner or a vibrant social setting, CHN Café delivers both.
