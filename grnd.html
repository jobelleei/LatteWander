GRND COFFEE

GRND coffee, Gatuslao St, Bacolod, 6100 Negros Occidental
@grnd.bcd
Café Background

GRND Coffee, located in Bacolod City, offers a modern and stylish coffee shop experience, particularly popular among young professionals and coffee enthusiasts. Known for its specialty coffee and chic industrial aesthetic, GRND creates an inviting atmosphere perfect for social gatherings, study sessions, or casual hangouts. The café stands out with its selection of high-quality beverages and light meals, emphasizing its commitment to delivering a premium coffee experience. Positioned in Mandalagan, GRND has become a go-to destination for those seeking both quality and ambiance in Bacolod's thriving coffee scene​.
