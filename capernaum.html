ANHI Café x Bistro, 27 corner Lacson Street, BS Aquino Dr, Bacolod
@anhicafexbistro
Café Background

Capernaum & Co. is a cozy café and coworking space located at the ESJ Building, 6th Lacson Street, Bacolod City, Philippines. Known for its inviting ambiance and delicious coffee, it provides an excellent venue for both work and leisure. The establishment features glass-partitioned cubicles, making it ideal for private study sessions or small meetings. Open from 8 AM to 8 PM, Monday through Saturday, it has become a favorite spot for students, professionals, and coffee enthusiasts alike. Capernaum & Co. also caters to private events, blending a relaxed atmosphere with functional facilities.
